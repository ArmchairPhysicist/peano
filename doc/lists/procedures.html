covariancestatisticsFunction

Returns the covariance of two vectors \(x\) and \(y\), $$ cov(x,y) =\frac{1}{n-1} \Sum_{i=1}^n \left(x_i - \mu_x \right) \left(B_i-\mu_y\right) $$

factorialstatisticsFunction

Returns the factorial of the scalar value \(x\), $$ x! = n(n-1)(n-2)\cccdot(3)(2)(1) $$

meanstatisticsFunction

Returns the arithmatic mean of a vector. $$ \mu = \frac{1,n} \Sum_{i=1}^n x_i $$

medianstatisticsFunction

Returns the median of a vector. If the vector has an odd number of elements, the "mean of middle" two is returned.

standardDeviationstatisticsFunction

Returns the sample standard deviation of a vector \(x\), $$ s = \sqrt{\frac{1}{n-1} \Sum_{i=1}^n \left(x_i - \mu_x \right)^2} $$

variancestatisticsFunction

Returns the sample variance of the vector \(x\), $$ s^2 = \frac{1}{n-1} \Sum_{i=1}^n \left(x_i - \mu_x \right)^2 $$
